HOW TO PLAY

The objective of the game is to match all the pairs of cards.

Click on a card to reveal it's Value. Then click another card to try to find the matching pair. If not the cards will flip back.

Your goal is to match all the pairs in the fewest moves and shortest time!

Good Luck!
